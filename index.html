ABOUT

Welcome to Ami’s Art Café, a cozy creative space where art meets coffee.
Located in the heart of the city, our café is designed for dreamers, artists, and coffee lovers who enjoy a calm and inspiring atmosphere.
At Ami’s, you can enjoy freshly brewed coffee, delicious pastries, and light meals while surrounded by beautiful artwork from local artists. We also host painting sessions, poetry nights, and live acoustic music to bring the community together.
Whether you’re here to relax, create, study, or connect with friends, Ami’s Art Café is the perfect place to feel inspired.
